# Neural Threads — 30-Second Hackathon Pitch

---

**Neural Threads is an AI personal stylist that helps you decide what to wear and what to donate.**

**Problem:** You have a closet full of clothes but still stare at it every morning. And half of it hasn't been worn in months.

**What we built:** A mobile app where you:
1. **Upload photos** of your clothes — AI classifies each item (type, color, style, season).
2. **Ask "What should I wear today?"** — Our stylist agent suggests outfits from your wardrobe based on your style, the season, and optional inspiration.
3. **Get declutter suggestions** — In-season items you haven't worn in 30+ days? We suggest donating them so someone else can use them, with friendly explanations.

**Tech:** React Native, FastAPI, Google Gemini Vision + Text, Open-Meteo for weather, SQLite.

**Demo:** Load the demo wardrobe, pick a style, tap "Get outfit suggestions" — AI returns outfit combos with natural-language explanations. Try the Declutter tab to see donation suggestions.

**30 seconds.**

---

## 5–10 minute demo (presentation)

1. **Intro (1 min)** — Problem: closet full of clothes, decision fatigue, unworn items.
2. **Wardrobe (1–2 min)** — Show grid; "Load demo wardrobe" or add one item via Upload (camera/gallery → AI classification).
3. **Stylist (2–3 min)** — Age + style + optional inspiration → "Get outfit suggestions". Show top 3 outfits with item images and reasons.
4. **Declutter (1–2 min)** — Open Declutter tab: "In-season items you haven't worn in 30+ days." Show suggestions and friendly explanations.
5. **Wrap (1 min)** — Tech stack, what's next, Q&A.
